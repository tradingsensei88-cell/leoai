VivaReady Principles.

Quality

01

Every question VivaReady generates is grounded in the actual content of your lab manual. We never hallucinate or fabricate — if it's not in your document, we don't ask about it.

Reliability

02

VivaReady remembers your answers across every session. Your gap map is always up to date, so every practice session picks up exactly where you left off — no repetition, no wasted time.

Creativity

03

Our adaptive questioning engine learns which concepts you consistently misapply and generates novel angles on those topics. Rote memorisation is replaced by genuine conceptual understanding.

Ambition

04

We measure success in student outcomes, not platform metrics. Our research roadmap is driven by what the evidence says actually improves viva pass rates — not what looks good on a product slide.
