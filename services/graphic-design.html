Four-stage Process.

Of course, every graphic design project is different, but broadly speaking we use a similar methodology — a four-stage process that’s designed to maximise your creative involvement while minimising unnecessary demands on your time.

First Stage

Clarify

We kick off the process with an in-depth review of your requirements, ideas and vision for the project. It’s a vital phase of information gathering and discussion which allows us to tightly focus our creative input and accelerate the design process — saving time and cost later.

Second Stage

Conceptualise

With all the building blocks in place, it’s time for our creative team to work their magic — drawing up design concepts to bring life to your initial ideas. Depending on the output this may involve sketches, wireframes or digital mockups, which we’ll discuss with you in detail to gather feedback.

Third Stage

Evolve

Taking your comments on board, we’ll refine and polish the concept to arrive at a final design mockup. At this stage, we’ll work closely with you to identify any tweaks, improvements or adjustments needed to take the project from 99% to completion — ensuring the output reflects and builds on your initial vision.

Final Stage

Implement

Once we’re all on the same page, it’s time to unveil the finished product. Thanks to our iterative design process and collaborative toolkit, we can move quickly from signoff to deliverable. This is particularly important for physical media — avoiding any delay to critical production or print deadlines.

Frequently asked questions

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to find the answers you've been looking for, without even having to ask.

How much do your design packages cost?

Design costs vary according to scope, format, print specifications, materials and more — so it’s difficult to give a guide price. Get in touch to discuss your needs, and we’ll create a custom estimate that includes the services relevant to your project and is costed based on a one-off or subscription model. You’ll find full details of our pricing structure here: Phunk Pricing

Do you offer graphic design services for social media?

Yes, we do. We can create compelling visuals that will enhance your brand's presence on social media platforms, driving engagement and building a cohesive brand narrative. From profile pictures to post graphics and story designs, we've got your social media branding covered.

Can you provide print-ready graphics?

Absolutely. We deliver all graphics in print-ready formats, ensuring a seamless transition from on-screen designs to physical assets. This means you can take our designs straight to the printer without any additional hassle or adjustments needed. Or, we can arrange printing and shipping for you, via one of our trusted partners.

What graphic design services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of graphic design services, including logo design, brand identity development, signage, packaging, and print design. We're your one-stop shop for all things design. From initial concept sketches to the final deliverable, we'll support you through the entire design journey.
