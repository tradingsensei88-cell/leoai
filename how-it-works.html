How It Works

From lab manual to exam-ready in 4 steps

Upload your document, practice with your AI examiner, close your knowledge gaps, and walk into your viva with genuine confidence.

01
Step 1
~30 seconds

Upload your lab manual

Drop your PDF, DOCX, or plain-text lab manual into Leo. Our RAG pipeline chunks, embeds, and indexes every page into a vector knowledge base — making the entire document searchable by meaning, not just keywords.

PDF / DOCX / TXT
Semantic chunking
Encrypted & private
02
Step 2
Instant

AI examiner begins questioning

Your AI examiner retrieves the most relevant chunks from your indexed document and generates authentic viva-style questions — the kind a real examiner would ask. It starts broadly, then narrows in based on your material and responses.

Viva-style questions
Retrieval-augmented
Zero hallucinations
03
Step 3
Continuous

Gaps are tracked & targeted

Every answer you give feeds into your personal knowledge-gap map. Topics you've mastered turn green. Topics you struggled with stay red and the AI automatically increases question frequency until you answer them correctly three sessions in a row.

Live gap dashboard
Adaptive re-testing
Full visibility
04
Step 4
Exam day

Walk in exam-ready

By the time your viva arrives, you've already answered every question your examiner could realistically ask — and you've proven mastery on the topics you once struggled with. No surprises. No anxiety. Just confidence backed by evidence.

Proven mastery

Zero surprises
